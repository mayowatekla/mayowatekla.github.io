Take Action

image by markus spiske on unsplash

Here are six simple steps you can take to help our planet!

Reduce, reuse, repair, recycle: Instead of buying new clothes from fast fashion chains, repair what you already have or donate it to charity. Buying second hand is a great way to find new things while also helping the planet.

When travelling to a nearby destination, walk, bike, or take public transportation instead of your car. This helps reduce the use of fossil fuels.

Save energy at home: When leaving a room, unplug an item that you don’t have a use for and turn off the light. Speaking of light, LED light bulbs are a good substitute as they last longer. When buying new electrical appliances, check how energy efficient they are and choose the most energy efficient.

Reduce food waste: Do not cook more food than you need to unless you plan on having leftovers. Instead of grocery shopping everyday, shop for a week. Planning meals in advance is helpful. Buy produce you know you will use to reduce waste.

This one might be a bit controversial. Switching out meat for a plant based substitute once a week can have a big impact. The meat industry makes up a big portion of greenhouses gases and a small adjustment to your lifestyle can be very beneficial to the planet.

Most importantly, don’t forget to recycle. Plastic bottles can be used for a variety of things such as making new bottles, jeans, car parts, playground equipment, etc. Small changes can have big impacts!
