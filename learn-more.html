Global Warming

You may have heard of greenhouses gases, but what does it mean? A greenhouse is used to protect plants inside from the cold outside. The Earth’s atmosphere does the same thing and it’s known as the greenhouse effect. Energy from the sun enters the atmosphere and warms the planets surface before being reflected towards space. However, the gases in the atmosphere prevent some of that heat from leaving, making the Earth warmer. Those gases are known as greenhouses gases. Most occur from the burning of fossil fuels. Some common ones are CO2 and CH4.
